✅ How to Use This Checklist
  1. Select your platform (iOS or Android) below
  2. Open the CT app on your phone alongside this checklist
  3. Work through each scenario — tap to expand sections
  4. For each test:
    • Pass ✓ — It worked as expected
    • Fail ✗ — Something went wrong (you'll be asked for details)
    • Skip – — You couldn't test this (e.g., feature not available)
  5. Submit your results when done — they go directly to our tracking sheet
🐛 When Something Fails

If you mark a test as Fail, you'll see fields to describe:

  • What happened (required)
  • What you expected to happen (required)
  • Severity level
